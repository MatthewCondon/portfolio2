Fire Extinguisher

The goal of this project was to become comfortable with random walks and arrays. The rules are very simple: hold down the mouse to activate the fire extinguisher. While it is on, bring it closer to fire to put it out.

The hardest part of this project was the fading aspect of putting out the fire. I did not want it to simply disappear as a whole. So, I worked out the mechanics to make it slowly disappear by using transparency values of rectangles.

If I were to refactor this project, I would want the flames and smoke to disappear in a smoother manner. I also would like to have the ash smoking.

Quick, put out the fire!
