Faces 2

This mini-project was designed to experiment with mouse clicking. The faces' designs are random. Furthermore, every time you click the screen, the amount of faces on the screen changes.

The hardest part of this project was getting the faces and emoji to have roughly the same amount. Due to size differences, the the amounts don't always equal the same, so they are dependent upon distance from the left side.

If I were to refactor this project, I would want the displayed emoji to change when the mouse is clicked.

I hope you like clicking through the faces!
