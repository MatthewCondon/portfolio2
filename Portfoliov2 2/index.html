About Me
I was born in Minnesota and have spent my
entire life in the state. I live at home with my
mother and wiener dog, Moby. My older brother
is an officer in the United States Coast Guard.

A senior at Rosemount High School, my favorite
class is Advanced Computer Science. I also
thoroughly enjoy Math classes. I plan on continuing
my passion for Computer Science and studying it
during college with an emphasis on cybersecurity.
Though I think I know where I will end up for
college, I am not quite ready to make the final decision.

I participate in a variety of activities at Rosemount
High School, placing an emphasis on academics,
athletics, and the arts. I am a captain of the Cross
Country team and member of the Track & Field team.
I am also involved with Speech and Debate, serving as
a captain in Speech. Furthermore, I am a committee
leader on Student Council and an officer in National
Honor Society. I am also heavily involved in Scouts of
America, having earned Eagle Scout. I also partake
in community service quite often.

I have come a long way in my Computer Science journey.
As a freshman and sophomore, I lacked profound interest
in the subject. However, after two years of my teacher
convincing me to try it, I ended up loving it. During two
years, I have taken A.P. Computer Science A and
currently take Advanced Computer Science (Data
Structures). I am comfortable coding in HTML and CSS,
Java, and JavaScript. I am also learning Python, C++,
and SQL when I have free time.
